The Best Movies Of All Time

Risa's Favourite Movies.

The Lord of the Rings Return of the King

The tale of Frodo Bagins comes to an end in this epic 3+ hour movie.
I literally can watch it on repeat without any issue.

Interstellar

Another epic movie. The human race needs a new home and space and time travel are the only solutions to this problem.
A terrific cast and Hans Zimmer's scores made this movie magical!

The Hobbit, The Desolation of Smaug

Back to the world of elves and dwarves and magic and swords. By this time, you've come to realize that I am indeed a nerd.
Though this is the second of The Hobbit's three installments, it is my favourite because of the glorious dragon, Smaug.
